Residues¶

class chemfiles::Residue¶

A groupement of atoms in the same logical unit.

A Residue is a group of atoms belonging to the same logical unit. They can be small molecules, amino-acids in a protein, monomers in polymers, etc.

Iterating over a Residue gives the indexes of the atoms in the associated Topology.
